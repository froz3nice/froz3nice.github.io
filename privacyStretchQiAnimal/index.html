Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Stretch: Qi Gong & Animal Flow App collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect personal information such as your name or contact details. Workout routines you save are stored locally on your device or in your chosen cloud service (iCloud or Google Drive) and are not accessed by the developer.

Cloud Storage

If you choose to save routines to iCloud or Google Drive, your data is subject to the privacy policies of those services. The app does not access or share your cloud data with third parties.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely on your device or in your cloud account. We do not collect or transmit your data to our servers.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated. Any changes will be posted on this website.
